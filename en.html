This is Atta Holding

A prominent UAE-based conglomerate, operates across diverse industries including Oil & Gas Lifting Equipment, Testing, and Advertising. As an early entrant to the UAE & GCC market, the company has established itself as an vital contractor in each field. in the manufacturing field, ATTA Holding offers locally manufactured products alongside reputable international principals, with a strong focus on meeting evolving customer needs while benefiting the community and environment.

Our goals exceed beyond being a dominant contractor in each field of operation, we embrace nationalisations, inclusivity and traditions. Our ultimate goal is to cultivate principles of inclusivity, sustainability and growth for both the ATTA teams and our stakeholders.

Our History

Our History
Atta emerged as one of the pioneering names in the oil & gas industry in the region, specializing in the provision of trading services and catering for several areas including the maritime and construction sectors.
