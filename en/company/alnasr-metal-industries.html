Alnasr Metal Industries

AMI stands at the forefront of the metal industry, boasting expertise in two key sectors: machining and fabrication. In the machining sector, we specialize in precision engineering for Oil Country Tubular Goods (OCTG), ensuring top-notch quality and reliability in every product. Our fabrication sector operates as a metal fabricating facility, crafting an array of products including baskets and steel structures. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, AMI delivers tailored metal solutions to meet the diverse needs of the Oil & Gas sector
